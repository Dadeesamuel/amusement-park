DINO'S

The Dino’s is one of our favorite kiddie rides, it is beautiful in design and with our LED lights at night it is a bright spot on the midway. 3 Tickets per rider Minimum Height allowed is 36″ Maximum Height allowed is 48″ This is a Children Only allowed ride



PONY CART

This is an original circle ride from the early days of Coney Island famous manufacturer W.F. Mangels, a German immigrant that started making rides in the 1880’s and the company continued until 1971. The original old fashioned crank system on the wheels causes the ponies to Gallup with your child in the cart. 2 Tickets per rider Minimum Height requirement is 30″ Maximum Height allowed is 50″ This is a Children Only ride- no adults or big kids over 75 pounds! All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.



DIZZY DRAGON

The Dizzy Dragons are a fun ride for kids, the ride spins in a circle as well as each dragon spins as you ride in the belly of the happy beast! It’s enough movement to make you dizzy….. 3 Tickets per rider Minimum Height Requirement is 36″ Children between 36″ and 42″ must have an adult rider with them All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.



PUPPY ROLL

Most people love a carnival and puppies, so we created the best of both worlds! A fun ride with lots of spinning, but at a puppy’s speed. Great for all ages in your family to ride for a thrill. 3 Tickets per rider Riders 36” and over



TEA CUPS

The Tea Cups is a classic family ride for all ages. You control the spin and the ride spins. 3 Tickets per rider Minimum Height allowed is 36″ Children can ride alone. All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.
