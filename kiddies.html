Kiddies Rides

For all of our youngest (and smallest) carnival goers, Kiddie Land is the perfect place to experience all the carnival fun! From the classic Mangel Fire Engine, and Pony Cart rides to the colorful spinning teacups, there are so many fun rides your little ones can enjoy. Height requirements are listed on each ride for your convenience.

Attractions, Amusement Rides, Carnival Games and Food at Acedu Amusements

View our classic, updated, refurbished, and out-of-this-world rides that you will see on our midways. We also have a variety of food and games that adds to the carnival experience! Dreamland Amusements has some extra amenities to make our customers feel as comfortable as possible and enjoy their day at the carnival to make memories that will last a lifetime.

Disclaimer: The rides you see below may not be at every location.

*We only service along the East Coast.

BUMPER CARS

We all remember bumping cars with our friends or cousins when we were in our school years. It is one of the classic rides where you can show mom or dad how to drive and crash, and not get a ticket! 4 Tickets per rider Minimum Height Requirement: 42″ With Adult: 42-48″ 48″ and above may drive alone All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.



CYCLONE

The Round UP is a fun gravity ride that will spin you in a circle very fast and then raise up on a tilt so you are spinning at an angle. Not for the faint, definitely for the thrill seeker! 4 Tickets per rider Minimum Height 46″ All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.



GLASS FIRE-HOUSE

Our Firehouse themed glass walk thru is a beautiful attraction, and fun to get through the maze. Mom and Dad can go too! 4 Tickets per rider Minimum Height Requirement: 36″ All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.



GRAND CAROUSEL

3 Tickets per rider No Cell phones, hats, glasses, purses, loose accessories, or loose shoes allowed while riding. We have bins to place your belongings. All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.



JUMBOS

The Jumbos are a family favorite that gives an uplifting ride experience to families in the most safe fun ever at a carnival. Each individual individual elephant rises as the ride circles. Great ride for a good family photo! Children between 38″ and 48″ must have an adult rider with them. 48″ and above can ride alone Minimum Height Requirement: 38″. All information provided here is subject to change at any time by our management team.
